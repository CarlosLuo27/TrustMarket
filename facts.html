Claim: Grocers are price-gouging.

Fact: Verified margins are only 1.5% - 2% (Agri-Food Lab, U of Guelph).

Claim: Inflation is driven by greed.

Fact: Main causes include supply chain disruptions and weather events.

Claim: Shrinkflation is corporate greed.

Fact: Shrinkflation often reflects cost pressures from suppliers and transport, not just profit-seeking. (StatsCan, 2023)

Claim: Canadian grocers made record profits during inflation.

Fact: Average grocery margins remained stable at 1.5%–2% across 2022–2023. (Agri-Food Analytics Lab)

Claim: Food prices are inflated due to middlemen adding fees.

Fact: Most markups occur from global shipping, not local logistics. (OECD Food Chain Report)

Claim: Egg prices soared due to corporate greed.

Fact: Bird flu outbreaks in major producers like the U.S. and Canada were the key drivers. (FAO, 2022)

Claim: Organic foods are always healthier.

Fact: Organic status refers to farming methods—not necessarily higher nutritional value. (Harvard Health Publishing)
